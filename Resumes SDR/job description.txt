Botsplash is seeking a motivated Sales Development Representative (SDR) to help generate qualified sales opportunities for our growing Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform.

The ideal candidate is proactive, curious, and highly self-driven, with strong communication skills and an interest in B2B software sales. This role will focus on prospect research, outbound outreach, lead pre-qualification, and scheduling demo calls for the sales team.

**Responsibilities**

* Research and build prospect lists across target industries such as fintech, lending, insurance, automotive, and education
* Reach out to prospects through phone, email, and other communication channels
* Pre-qualify leads by understanding business needs, pain points, and potential fit for Botsplash
* Schedule demo calls and hand off qualified opportunities to the sales team
* Use the Botsplash platform to engage, follow up, and re-engage cold and warm prospects
* Work closely with marketing to support campaigns, prospect lists, and lead generation efforts
* Assist with direct mail campaigns, event follow-up, and other top-of-funnel sales activities
* Collaborate with sales, marketing, operations, and product teams to improve outreach and demo conversion
* Bring new ideas and processes to improve lead generation and sales pipeline growth

**Requirements**

* 4 year of experience in sales or lead generation
* Strong written and spoken English communication skills
* Career focus in B2B SaaS sales and technology
* Ability to understand prospect pain points and communicate how Botsplash can help
* Comfortable making outbound calls, sending emails, and following up with prospects
* Self-motivated, organized, and able to manage daily outreach activity independently
* Collaborative, coachable, and eager to learn from the team

**Work Requirements**

* Full-time role
* Must maintain at least 5 hours of overlap with US Eastern Time
* Based in Hyderabad but open to across India for qualified candidates
* Remote work environment

**Benefits**

* Competitive pay
* Fully remote work environment
* Opportunity to grow in B2B SaaS sales
* Hands-on training with sales, marketing, and customer engagement tools
* Career growth into account executive, sales operations, customer success, or marketing roles

**About Botsplash**

Botsplash helps businesses “Be Where Their Customers Are.” Our Software-as-a-Service platform enables companies to manage customer conversations across multiple digital channels, including SMS, website chat, video, social messaging, and more.

We work with clients across fintech, lending, insurance, education, automotive, and other industries. Botsplash is a bootstrapped, profitable company with a collaborative team focused on helping businesses improve customer engagement through better conversations.
